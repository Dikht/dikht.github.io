Landing Page is a one-page marketing site designed to promote your products and skillss on the Internet. It is a personal manager who will advantage the product instead of you day and night.

The main purpose of this site is to convince a potential customer to make an order by leaving a request on your site. We will help you create beautiful, user-friendly, and above all, profitable page.

This order was received from a repairman, whose goal was to create a website with all his advantages shown and convincing the client to choose him. We decided to develop a Landing page bringing all the necessary information and increasing the number of requests.

On home page, the user sees the representative of the company and the benefits available.

Further appears the information on the skillss with minimalistic design. After scrolling, the user can learn about the work and ensure the reliability of cooperation.

Business card website is an online presentation for your company. This kind of site usually contains from one to five pages with information about the company: images, description of goods, skillss and contact information. This site is like the business card of the company, but more detailed, aimed to present your company in a best way.

Aurel Furniture Factory has proven itself as a stable company able to produce comfortable and practical furniture. Our goal was to create a simple but impressive business card site, which could show the work of the factory at its best.

Once on the home page, the user can immediately mark capabilities of the company, thanks to the large banner.

A small menu in the header reduces the search for necessary information. The compactness and simplicity of the good cards allows placing an order quickly and easily choosing desired color and configuration of the product.

Modern trends require special attention to the information that the customers see. That is why it should not only be clear and competent, but also visually pleasing. The site includes a fixed header, which makes its usage easier.

The most important thing here is the interest, that user feels visiting this vibrant and active website. He will definitely read all information to the end of the page.

Corporate websites are multi-page sites with information about the company’s functions. It is designed in a corporate style which strictly follows the line of your company’s work.

Privat Bank is one of the largest banks in Ukraine. We developed a website that would meet all the requirements of the company. The key features of the site are the simplicity and clarity.

Following the corporate identity, we used green and grey colors.

Convenient arrangement of important components.

Feedback opportunity has been presented.

For 4shops website we used infographic reducing the amount of information, which greatly simplified the perception of the site. Every minute counts for customers who carry out transfers. For this reason, we tried to replace text with visual information.

A large banner background on the home page shows the user that he got on the transportation carrier’s website.

Going forward we have clearly defined what are the benefits of the company in an understandable and enjoyable way for the customer. Footer is made in a minimalistic style.
