Why to hire us?

Nowadays it’s no secret that there is a vast variety of web design studios, so each client is faced with a hard choice to make: where to create a website. Today we offer our reader to get acquainted with the advantages of our studio.

The first thing should be noted, that work on any project begins with the client’s brief. It is a special form sent to the client, which has all the necessary questions to clarify the requirements for the web product. This document will not only help our team develop an action plan accurately, but also useful the customer to determine the desires and requirements to the final product on the concept stage.

After filling in the brief, our managers will study it in details and prepare an individual development plan, which in the future will be strictly followed by the whole team.

After the approval of the brief, our customers receive the unique product of our studio – the online functionality form. This web document is a detailed description of each function of your future site, which is divided into several blocks. Each function belongs to one of the sections representing kinds of sites: Landing Page, a business card site, corporate site, online store, responsive websites and additional features-widgets.

You will be able to decide on the kind of site without assistance remotely, sitting at your computer. Gradually, by studying the possible types of functionality, you can choose the desired functions for the site, clearly understanding their purposes and abilities.

In most cases, the client is not given such a detailed description of the functionality and is simply offered a complete package, which may not always be suitable for the client's ideas. For example, studios usually offer online business card, when you must make a corporate website. The differences between the kinds of sites can be viewed here. Our studio does not limit the customer, and offers you to examine all possible functionality and add to your site what you need exactly. If desired, you can always consult with our experts. This approach helps make the site more intuitive and suitable for the user. It is not overloaded with unnecessary elements.

Then, after filling the online functionality form, the data is received and processed, so that you will be able to find out the cost of your site. This approach is modern and comfortable. Our studio is one of the few where the customer feels the benefits and clearly understands what he orders.

After functional is approved, we can announce terms of performance of the task. Next comes the stage of signing the contract and the prepayment. The rest of the payment you make after the fulfillment of all specified works. We draw your attention to the fact that our team will prepare instructions on the use of the website for your convenience. We will do everything for your convenience.
