What kind of website to choose for your business?

Any businessman who wants to learn how to promote his business on the Internet, thinks about creating a marketing site. However, after reading a few articles, you will certainly find out that you need to be aware of your target audience, but also be familiar with the kinds of sites. We have decided to lift the curtain and talk about the differences between some types of sites.

One of the easiest and fast to build is Landing Page. It is a powerful instrument for advertising goods and services. Actually, it is long one-page site, bright and strictly informative it encourages the visitor to make a purchase. Such sites usually contain buttons for quick order or feedback form to get the contact information from the visitor. Landing Page would be a perfect solution for those who want to invite people to the training, to familiarize the audience with the service, or to obtain higher rates for sales of certain goods.

For those who provide services involving portfolio, the ideal option would be online business card. This site is a multi-page, as it contains additional information tabs. The site can be decorated with a wide colorful banner that will attract attention and news feeds in order to inform customers about the updates in the company, as well as to promote the site in the internet.

Corporate website is more complicated form of a business card. It may contain multifunctional directory of pages with treelike structure, site search and even a personal user account.

If you want to create not just an advertising site, but a large-scale shop on the Internet, you should definitely get acquainted with the sites such as online stores of internet markets. Their implementation is the most complex but it surely worth it as they are also among the most popular and successful types of websites for selling products on the Internet. The main difference of this site is a "shopping cart". It is developed carefully taking into account all features of the claimed goods and allows your customers to make purchases without leaving home. If you have a wish, you can extend the functionality of an online store, adding a variety of stocks and bonuses to attract and increase sales.

You can always refer to specialists of our web studio, who will help determine the most suitable kind for your business website.
