UIcodes VS WebPanda

It is no secret that any original product has its own prehistory. Our studio is not an exception. So today we will talk about how the name UIcodes came up, and also a choice of color scheme for our web studio.

Initially, our studio had a name WebPanda. The name was given by one of the founders - Evhen Volynets. Evhen established himself as a successful teacher, webmaster and a good hand at his profession.

Panda caught Evhen’s eye from the first glance, because this lovely animal is capable of spending a lot of time every day finding food of the best quality, even if it takes a lot of effort. Just like pandas, our team is able to offer each customer the right solution, because we do not focus on a typical performing of the similar tasks. We always sort out only the best of the best in order to solve the task not only in the short terms, but also to deliver the desired results.

Pandas always prefer fresh, comfortable and pleasant place for their lives. Therefore, the decision has been made to create a vivid, but at the same time rather calm and fresh look for our website. Each page is accompanied by elements of nature, which favorably complement the overall concept of the site.

After some time and negotiation, it was concluded to rename the web studio in UIcodes, to highlight the main direction of the team - creation of websites, as well as mobile versions of Internet products. Our studio is different from many others because of the contemporary approach to development services, thanks to another founder of the team, Alex Bidnichenko, who is a multilingual developer with rich long-term experience in programming both web and mobile applications.

Nevertheless, we couldn’t certainly give up on pandas. There is a panda, whose name is UI. We decided to use it as the basis for the name of the studio, as the UI stands for a user interface in the web development world. Adding to that, we use flexible and the most appropriate ways of writing programming code, that’s the reason why the second part of the name was - codes. Elegant combination of perfectly suitable words brought UIcodes to life.

Now you can get a modern, completely original approach in creating web content and solving the tasks, because our studio is a team of young professionals with a high level of knowledge and skills.
